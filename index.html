The road to decentralization

Introducing the MEOW DAO

At the core of Toshi's evolution is the MEOW DAO, a testament to our dedication to decentralized governance and community empowerment. A DAO, or Decentralized Autonomous Organization, allows members to vote on decisions democratically and transparently.

Toshi is transitioning into a fully decentralized DAO, where holders of Toshi tokens will have voting rights, emphasizing community-driven governance. The MEOW DAO signifies that Toshi is more than a project; it is a community initiative that enhances the utility and involvement of token holders. The MEOW DAO is not only embracing the ethos of decentralization but also ensuring that Toshi remains at the forefront of community-led innovation and governance.

Facts about Toshi

The Face of Base

What is Toshi?

$TOSHI is a memecoin named after Coinbase co-founder Brian Armstrong's cat and the pseudonymous creator of Bitcoin, Satoshi Nakamoto. It serves as the face and mascot of Base, the Ethereum aligned, layer-2 blockchain created by Coinbase.

Fun fact: Did you know Coinbase Wallet was originally named Toshi? The lore goes deep, anon.

What does Toshi aim to be?

Toshi aims to become the Face of Base and a leading figure in the burgeoning Base ecosystem. Leveraging a robust meme culture and an expanding community, Toshi strives to be the premier memecoin on Base, drawing in the masses with its significant utility and industry-recognized brand to onboard billions onchain.

What are NFToshis?

NFToshis are Toshi’s exclusive line of NFTs. Initially offered as a free mint to Toshi token holders, NFToshis now symbolize the identity of community members in the Toshi ecosystem. You can view this collection on Rarible.

What are NFToshis 2.0?

NFToshis 2.0 isn’t just a new collection; it’s a complete overhaul designed to offer a more engaging and visually appealing experience. The collection will consist of 5,000 unique NFTs, each carefully crafted to embody the spirit of Toshi’s rebranding. Whether you’re an OG holder or new to the Toshi community, NFToshis 2.0 offers something special for everyone.

The NFToshi 2.0 collection is available on MagicEden.

Why does Toshi have the MEOW DAO?

Toshi is a project driven by its community, always seeking its members' input on key decisions. The MEOW DAO empowers Toshi community members to influence important decisions by voting with their tokens—each token represents one vote. This approach embodies the essence of a community-led project.
